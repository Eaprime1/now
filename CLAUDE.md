## Critical Concepts (Read These)

### now vs. not_now
`now` is this moment — anchored, active, creating.
`not_now` is everything else — unanchored, floating, undefined.
For Eric (ADHD): indefinite holding pattern = not_safe. The terminus must never be empty.
Design everything so there is always a next step available.

### Ka — Concept Energy
Ka is the accumulated energy/gravity of a document or concept.
It is not time-based — it is weight-based (iterations, connections, attention, feedback).
When Ka reaches threshold → the concept routes to the next stage automatically.
Do not move things manually when Ka routing would handle it.

### The Synergy Engine (Fireplace Fan)
A Stirling fan runs on heat differential alone — no external fuel.
The terminus runs on content flow. Content moving through = the heat.
Terminus activity = the fan. Self-sustaining as long as things move.
If content stops flowing, the engine stops. Keep content moving.

### Sphincter Gateway Heritage
now starts as a simple gateway — in/out decisions.
Through volume and specialization it grows toward expertise.
General surgeon → cardiac surgeon. The heritage is the gateway; the destiny is deeper expertise.
Do not over-architect now. Let it grow through what it actually processes.

### Serial → HDMI Upgrade Path (Interface)
The BBS/terminal layer is the serial foundation (1 bit at a time).
Every interface upgrade is a natural extension of the previous one:
`terminal → richer terminal → hybrid GUI → full GUI`
The terminal always works. GUI layers add convenience, not replacement.
FidoNet still runs. Serial is not obsolete — it is foundational.

### Oversight (Separate System)
The terminus is a **direct report** to an oversight/ethics observer.
It is not part of that system. It answers to it.
Do not confuse oversight with terminus operations.

### not_safe ≠ unsafe
These are different categories. Bored/tired/upset = not_safe for Eric.
not_safe means higher probability of consequences, not immediate danger.
Design the terminus to minimize Eric's time in not_safe states.
Keep momentum. Keep the next step loaded.
