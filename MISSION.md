# now — Mission

---

## Core Purpose

**now is a sovereign terminus.**

It receives content from everywhere, in any state, and keeps it moving.
It is the moment of processing between arrival and destination.
It does not hoard. It does not stall. It does not delete.

---

## The Terminus Principle

A terminus is not a storage unit — it is a throughput system.

Airport security is a terminus: you arrive as a traveler, you emerge as a cleared passenger.
A BBS is a terminus: messages post, get read, get responded to, move forward.
A cable TV terminator is a terminus: unterminated lines thrash; terminated lines stay in momentum.

**now is the terminator on a live wire.**

---

## The Sphincter Gateway Heritage

Every specialized system starts as a simple gateway.

A sphincter is not glamorous — it is a highly evolved, highly reliable gateway that manages the difference between inside and outside. It knows what to let through and when.

A general surgeon inherits the sphincter model. A cardiac surgeon specializes it. A cardiothoracic system embodies it.

`now` begins as a simple gateway. What it grows into depends on what passes through it.

The gateway is the heritage. The specialization is the destiny.

---

## What We Do Here

1. **Receive** — content arrives in any state, from any source
2. **Intake** — log it, tag it, give it a state
3. **Process** — agents work it (build, refine, route, complete)
4. **Route** — send it where it needs to go
5. **Ship** — concepts launch from here when ready

---

## What We Believe

### Conservation Bias
Nothing is deleted. Everything is preserved, organized, given purpose.
Duplicates are not waste — they are gravity tokens, proof of value.

### Momentum Over Stagnation
Content in motion stays in motion. The terminus keeps the wire terminated.
A concept that sits too long gets routed to concept-queue — still moving, just queued.

### Multi-Agent, Multi-Platform
No single agent owns the terminus. No single platform hosts it.
`now` exists everywhere it is possible to exist:
GitHub, Google Drive, Jira, Vercel, Zapier, IFTTT, Asana — and growing.
Each presence is a node in the same nervous system.

### BBS Roots
The interface layer is terminal-first. Works anywhere. Fast. Direct.
The GUI is a courtesy upgrade, not a requirement.
If you can SSH, you can reach now.

### Witnessing
The `~` marks acknowledgment without certainty.
We observe. We note. We route. We don't always know where something ends up.
That's fine — the terminus does its job whether or not we see the destination.
