## Key Crystallizations from This Session

### now = create / queue = work

Simple IS.

- `now` is the space of **creation** — making, building, being in the moment
- `concept-queue` is the space of **work** — processing, completing, routing
- These are not hierarchical — they are peers with different natures
- You don't work in now. You create in now. Work goes to queue.

### The Synergy Engine (Fireplace Fan Model)

A Stirling/fireplace fan runs on heat differential alone. No batteries. No external fuel. The fire's own heat spins the fan, which distributes the heat, which sustains the fire. Self-sustaining through the activity of the system itself.

Applied to terminus:
- The **flow of content** through now is the heat
- The **terminus activity** is the fan
- It keeps spinning as long as content moves
- It stops if content stops — which is why momentum is the core principle

The synergy engine is what keeps the terminus from becoming a storage unit.
Simple mechanism. Profound effect.

### not_now — The Unnamed Danger

Everything that is not happening in this moment is `not_now`.
`not_now` has no anchor. It floats.

For someone (Eric) who cannot tolerate doing nothing:
- Indefinite holding pattern = fish out of water
- Boredom is not_safe (note: not_safe ≠ unsafe — different categories)
- Bored / tired / upset = probability of bad idea increases
- Survival mode activates: nothing else in the universe matters except getting from A to B
- The stepping motor pattern handles one step: A→B. Then B auto-loads C. Then C→D.

Design implication: **the terminus must never be empty**. Content in motion is safe content.
A terminus with nothing to process is a fish out of water.
This is why concept-queue exists — even holding has structure.

### Stepping Motor / Daisy Wheel Pattern

Movement as a series of discrete steps:
- A → B (the step you can always handle)
- B auto-loads → C (next step is already prepared)
- Hardened path: "requires a great amount of Ka to impact"
- Momentum is the protection. Stagnation is the vulnerability.

Applied to content routing:
- Content always knows its next step
- The step is simple (one move)
- The next step is pre-loaded by the completion of this one
- You never need to know the whole path — just A→B

### The Serial → HDMI Upgrade Path

FidoNet (1984, older than Eric, still alive via telnet/TCP/IP).
The only limit was network speed. The protocol was fine.

Serial port pattern (1 bit at a time) gives the foundation:

```
Serial (1 bit)
  ↓ upgrade
Joystick port
  ↓ upgrade  
Parallel port / Centronics (1 byte at a time)
  ↓ upgrade
Audio → Video → S-Video
  ↓ upgrade
HDMI (full AV digital)
```

Each step is a natural upgrade of the previous one.
The BBS terminal is the serial layer.
The GUI is somewhere up the chain.
The triple helix with umbilical core is the wiring that grows through each upgrade.

Applied to BBS interface:
```
Terminal / Telegard (serial layer) — works NOW
  ↓
Richer terminal (parallel layer)
  ↓
Hybrid terminal/GUI (AV layer)
  ↓
Full GUI (HDMI layer)
```

You can run many things on the serial pattern. It's the universal foundation.
